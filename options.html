Undo Archive Shortcut

Click in the box below, then press the key combination you want to use (for example Ctrl + Z or Alt + U).

How it works

  • Where it runs: Only on Outlook web pages (outlook.live.com, outlook.office.com, outlook.office365.com).
  • What it does: Listens for your shortcut and clicks the Undo button that appears after you archive or move an email.
  • Typing safety: Shortcuts are ignored while you are typing in an input, text area, or editable field so they do not interfere with composing emails.
